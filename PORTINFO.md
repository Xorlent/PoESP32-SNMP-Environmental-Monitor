# M5Stack PoESP32 Port and Address Info
## GPIO
### Ethernet IP101G (SMI)
- MDC 23  
- MDIO 18  
- CLK 0  
- PWR 5  
- PHY ADDR 1  
### Ethernet IP101G (RMII)
- TX_EN 21  
- TX0 19  
- TX1 22  
- RX0 25  
- RX1 26  
- CRS_DV 27  
### I2C
- RX 16  
- TX 17  
## I2C DEVICES
### SHT40 Temp + Humidity
- Address 0x44  
### BMP280 Pressure
- Address 0x76
